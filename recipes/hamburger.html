Hamburger

Yummy Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill on high heat.
  2. Mix together ground beef, onion, cheese, onion soup mix, egg, garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until well-done, about 5 minutes per side.
